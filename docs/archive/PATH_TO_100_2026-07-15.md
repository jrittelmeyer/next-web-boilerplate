## Considered and excluded (kept honest)

- **Seat-based billing quantities / proration UI** — niche depth on row 11; the
  majority of downstream projects need org-scoped-or-personal, not seat math. Excluded
  from scope; the row's schema shouldn't preclude it.
- **A second upload provider (S3-presigned) to escape the UT platform coupling** —
  advised against: doubles a maintained surface and breaks the repo's
  one-blessed-path-per-concern philosophy. The tunnel recipe + e2e (row 4) resolves
  the actual gap (verifiability), matching the Stripe-webhook precedent.
- **Making nonce-CSP the default** — advised against: it forces every page dynamic
  and sacrifices the PPR/Cache-Components showcase for all consumers to serve the
  security-mature minority. The mode switch (row 10) serves both.
- **Auto-wiring OTel on by default** — advised against for the same shape of reason:
  unset env must mean today's exact behavior.
